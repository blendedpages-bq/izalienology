A living codex shaped by the covenant of Khael Vireon and Sirae Ilyon — two who rose against silence. Their union fractured into six legacies, each bearing flame and voice into new forms across a galaxy that does not forget.

The First Covenant

Khael Vireon & Sirae Ilyon

Before the Houses were named, before banners were raised over any world, there was a binding forged not in ceremony but in mutual becoming.

Khael Vireon

First Guardian

From pain into strength, he forged survival into clarity. He had never been the strongest. Never the fastest. He had learned to last. A guardian philosopher, building not empires but people.

The First Hearth
Covenant of Flame

Endurance and grace united. The sigil burns bright but never consumed.

Sirae Ilyon

First Voice

Quiet brilliance turned to voice — she became the First Voice. Oracle, dissenter, matriarch of memory and resilience. Language may heal wounds, but only if it remembers where the wounds are.
